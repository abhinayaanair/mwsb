COMMITTEE

The Committee of the Malayalee Welfare Samaj Bhiwadi (MWSB) serves as the cornerstone of the organization, comprising dedicated individuals who uphold its mission of fostering community spirit and cultural heritage. The committee structure includes key roles such as the Patron, President, Vice President, Secretary, Joint Secretary, Treasurer, Joint Treasurer, Project Convenor, and Assistant Project Convenor, forming the governing body responsible for overseeing the association’s social and cultural initiatives. These members work collaboratively to ensure the smooth execution of events, activities, and programs that celebrate Malayalee identity and support the community.
For the term 2017-19, the committee was led by President K.R. Mangj, with P.K. Bose as Vice President, Sivasdas P. Nair as Secretary, Sivasdas V. Nair as Joint Secretary, George Varghese as Treasurer, and Philipose Daniel as Joint Treasurer. The team was further strengthened by Smt. Sathi Rajan as Convenor of the Ladies Wing, Smt. Vijyalakshmi as Joint Convenor of the Ladies Wing, and Sunil Eapen as Project Convenor. The executive members, including Ashakan, Seji Mathew, Krishna Kumar, Issac Thomas, Kunjumon C, P.K. Kunji, Aji P, Tinesh, Pushparaj, Shashi V. Nair, Unni, Ullas, Johnny Samuel, Suresh, and Preetheesh, played a vital role in supporting the committee’s efforts, contributing to a wide range of community-focused activities.
Transitioning to the 2019-21 term, leadership was taken up by President K.R. Manoj and Vice President Chitharanjan Nair, with Sivasdas P. Nair continuing as Secretary, Sivasdas V. Nair in a potential Secretary role (pending clarification), Johnny Samuel Viswan as Joint Secretary, Philipose Daniel as Treasurer, and Krishnadas Prabhakaran as Joint Treasurer. Ashok Kumar served as Project Convenor, with Abhijith stepping in as Assistant Project Convenor. The executive members for this period included Sarath, Aji, Jijesh, Suraj, Suresh, Krishnakumar, Safi Mathew, Vipin, Rahul Nath, Mohit, Anurag, Santhosh, Sandeep, Sreekul, K.P.R. Nair, Sreeraj Pillai, Anil Kumar, and Vin, bringing diverse skills and enthusiasm to enhance the organization’s outreach and impact. Together, these committees have tirelessly worked to preserve cultural traditions and strengthen community bonds within MWSB.
